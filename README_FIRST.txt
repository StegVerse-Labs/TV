### ðŸ§  Core Operating Principles
The Token Vault is governed by a *Guardian-first* approach:

- **Least privilege** and **short-lived access**
- **Auditability and provenance** of actions affecting trust or security
- **Survivor-protective and whistleblower-safe** ethical alignment
- **Reversible, measurable, and accountable** operational design

> The role of a custodian is not to *control* access, but to **protect the people and truths that access can impact**.
